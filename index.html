The Final Flicker

Official Manual: Version 27.0 (Strict Edition)

This candle is your heartbeat. As long as it flickers, you are safe. But every card you burn brings you one step closer to being Consumed by the Darkness.

However, the dark isn't the only danger. If you let too many ghosts gather, you won't just die—you will lose your mind to the Possession.

Do not trust your neighbours. They are not your friends. They will shove the spirits into your shadow to save their own skin.

"You don't have to be the brightest light in the room. You just have to be the last one burning."

1. The Setup

The Supplies

Ratio: 1 Standard Deck (54 cards) for every 2 players.

  • IMPORTANT: Use strictly 2 Jokers per Deck.
  • 2-4 Players: 2 Decks
  • 5-6 Players: 3 Decks
  • 7-8 Players: 4 Decks
  • 9-20 Players: Scale accordingly (1 deck per 2 players)

The Shuffle: Shuffle all decks together to create one massive pool.

The Circle & The Start

  1. Sit in a circle.
  2. Deal exactly 27 cards (half a deck) to each player. This is your Candle.
  3. Place any remaining undealt cards Face Up into THE DARK (Discard Pile) to start the game.
  4. The Chilling Touch: All players must touch the hand of the person to their left. The player at the table with the Coldest Hands goes first.
  5. THE TWIN FLAME (For 6+ Players):
    If the group is large (6 or more), the player sitting directly opposite the starting player also begins a turn. Play proceeds clockwise from both points simultaneously.

4. The Passage of Time

Phase A: THE HAUNTING

Look into your Shadow. Count the Ghosts. For every Ghost currently haunting you, BURN 1 card from your Candle into The Dark.

Phase B: THE BURN

Draw 1 card from the top of your Candle.

Phase C: THE RITUAL

Perform EXACTLY ONE of the following:

  • HAUNT (Attack): Play a Number Card (A-10) into a Neighbour's Shadow.
  • BANISH (Defend): Play a card from hand onto a Ghost in your Shadow (Higher/Equal Value).
    THE SIPHON: If Banish Rank matches Ghost Rank, Heal the Ghost.
    EXCEPTION: You cannot Siphon Spades (♠).
    SUIT TIERS (Tie-Breaker): Spades > Hearts > Clubs > Diamonds.
  • PANIC (Desperation): Play the Top Card of your Candle BLINDLY.
    Higher/Equal: Success. Lower: Failure (Ghost Added).
    Face Cards (J,Q,K) count as VALUE 0.
    JOKER MIRACLE: Instant Win! You Siphon the Ghost (Heal) unless it is a Spade.
  • SÉANCE (Heal): Discard a MATCHING PAIR. Take BOTTOM 3 CARDS of The Dark to your Candle.
  • CAST (Effect) / SUMMON (Power): Discard for Ability (See Grimoire).

Phase D: THE BURDEN & CHECK

Discard down to 5 cards.

THE FINAL CHECK (POSSESSION):
Look at your Shadow. If you have 3 Ghosts of the SAME SUIT, you are Possessed and lose immediately.
