The Final Flicker

Official Manual: Version 3.4 (The Panic Update)

This candle is your heartbeat. As long as it flickers, you are safe. But every card you burn brings you one step closer to being Consumed by the Darkness.

However, the dark isn't the only danger. If you let too many ghosts gather, you won't just die—you will lose your mind to the Possession. You must balance your fading light against your crumbling sanity.

Do not trust your neighbours. They are not your friends. They will shove the spirits into your shadow to save their own skin.

"You don't have to be the brightest light in the room. You just have to be the last one burning."

1. The Setup

The Supplies

Ratio: 1 Standard Deck (54 cards) for every 2 players.

  • IMPORTANT: Use strictly 2 Jokers per Deck. If your decks have extra jokers, remove them.
  • 2-4 Players: 2 Decks
  • 5-6 Players: 3 Decks
  • 7-8 Players: 4 Decks
  • 9-20 Players: Scale accordingly (1 deck per 2 players)

The Shuffle: Shuffle all decks together to create one massive pool.

The Calling (Class Selection)

Before the game begins, every player must accept a curse.

The Circle & The Start

  1. Sit in a circle.
  2. Deal exactly 27 cards (half a deck) to each player.
  3. The Candle: This stack is your life. Do not look at it. If it runs out, you are Consumed by the Darkness.
  4. The Surplus: Place any remaining undealt cards Face Up into THE DARK (Discard Pile) to start the game.
  5. The Chilling Touch:
    All players must touch the hand of the person to their left. The player at the table with the Coldest Hands goes first, for the spirits are already drawn to them.
  6. The Twin Flame (For 6+ Players):
    If the group is large (6 or more), the player sitting directly opposite the starting player also begins a turn. Play proceeds clockwise from both points simultaneously.
  7. The Hand: Draw 3 cards from your Candle.
  8. The Shadow: The empty space on the table directly in front of you. This is where ghosts manifest.

2. The Laws of the Circle

  • Line of Sight: You can only interact with your Immediate Neighbours (Left/Right).
  • The Accumulation: Ghosts played against you gather in your personal Shadow.
  • Forbidden Knowledge: Cards are discarded Face Up, but you may never look through the history of The Dark (Discard Pile) unless a card tells you to.
  • The Closing Gap: When a player dies, they are removed. The circle shrinks.
    • Standard: Ghosts are banished to The Dark.
    • Dark Mode: Ghosts split between neighbours.

3. The Ritual Circle (Layout & Targeting)

Tracking Ghosts: When a Neighbour Haunts you, place the ghost card on the side of your Shadow closest to them.

4. The Passage of Time (Turn Structure)

Phase A: THE HAUNTING

Look into your Shadow. Count the Ghosts. For every Ghost currently haunting you, BURN (Discard Face Up) 1 card from your Candle into The Dark.

Phase B: THE BURN (Choose One)

  • OPTION 1 (STANDARD): Draw 1 card from your Candle.
  • OPTION 2 (FLICKER): Choose any number of cards from your Hand. Place them at the bottom of your Candle. Then, Draw that same number of cards.
    (You do not gain a +1 card advantage this turn, but you fix your hand).

If your Candle is empty when you try to Draw, you are Consumed by the Darkness.

Phase C: THE RITUAL

Perform EXACTLY ONE of the following:

  • HAUNT (Attack): Play a Number Card (A-10) into a Neighbour's Shadow.
    (Place it on the side of their shadow closest to you).
  • BANISH (Defend): Play a card from hand onto a Ghost in your Shadow.
    Requirement: Must be Higher or Equal value.
    The Tie-Breaker: If the numbers are Equal, your Suit must be Higher.
    THE SIPHON: If you Banish with the EXACT SAME NUMBER (Rank), you Heal the Ghost.
    EXCEPTION: You cannot Siphon Spades (♠). Spades are too corrupted and must always be destroyed.
  • PANIC (Desperation): If you cannot (or will not) Banish a ghost from your hand, you may play the Top Card of your Candle BLINDLY.
    Number Card: If the blind card is Higher/Equal, you survive. If Lower, you now have 2 Ghosts.
    CRITICAL SUCCESS: If you Panic and flip a Face Card (J,Q,K), it is an Instant Success. The Ghost is Banished AND you trigger the Face Card's power immediately!
  • SÉANCE (Heal): Discard a MATCHING PAIR of cards from your hand (e.g., two 5s). Do not resolve their effects.
    Instead, take the top 3 cards from The Dark (Discard Pile) and place them at the bottom of your Candle.
  • CAST (Effect): Discard a Number Card to use its Ability.
  • SUMMON (Power): Discard a Face Card to use its Power.

Phase D: THE BURDEN

If you have 6+ cards in hand, discard down to 5.

THE FINAL FLICKER CHEAT SHEET

Quick Reference Guide

Turn Structure

  1. THE HAUNTING: Count ghosts. Burn cards from Candle.
  2. THE BURN:
    - Draw 1 Card
    - OR Flicker: Cycle Hand to bottom of Candle, then Draw same amount.
  3. THE RITUAL: Perform ONE action:
    • Haunt: Play Number into Neighbour's Shadow.
    • Banish: Destroy ghost in own Shadow (Higher/Equal Value).
      Siphon: If Rank is Equal, Heal Ghost to Candle.
      (Cannot Siphon Spades).
    • Panic: Play Blind from Candle. Face Card = Crit Success.
    • Séance: Discard 2 of same Rank to Heal 3 from Dark.
    • Cast/Summon: Discard for Effect/Power.
  4. THE BURDEN: Discard down to 5 cards.

Endings

  • Consumed: 0 Cards in Candle.
  • Possession: End turn with 3 Ghosts of SAME SUIT in Shadow.

Card Effects

ADrain: Steal 1 Card from Foe's Candle.
2Scare: Foe Discards 1 RANDOM card.
3Omen: Reveal 3 from Candle, Play 1.
4Salt: Cancel an Action (Wastes Foe's Turn).
5Greed: Draw 2.
6Cleanse: Destroy Ghost. Siphon if Suits match.
7Sight: Steal 1 card from Foe.
8Vanish: Return Ghost to Hand.
9Possess: Move Ghost to Foe.
10Rekindle: Get 3 from Discard to Candle.
JMirror: Swap Ghosts with Foe.
QMedium: Fetch 1 from Dark OR Heal 2.
KPurge: Destroy ALL Ghosts (Self).
JokerHaunted: All Foes reveal Candle until they get 1 Ghost.
RULE: CORRUPTED SPADES ♠
Ghost Spades cannot be Siphoned (Healed). They must be destroyed.
